About Us

At Nepal Ventures we are ecstatic at the possibility of a fellow traveler joining us. We welcome you to Nepal where you will be an audience to a spectacle of nature and culture. We dedicate our resources and operations to serving our clients so that they can enjoy the most memorable time of their lives here in Nepal.

Our services are of the highest standard and are aimed to offer the warmest of hospitality and the most comfortable of journeys. We have numerous travel options in Nepal including trekking, tour, hiking, mountaineering, and adventure sports. On all of these packages, we offer skilled and experienced experts to guide you through the trip.

Additionally, we also have a number of lucrative discounts on a large number of these trips. Along with travel and business, we are also concerned about the environment and the state of the heritage that we are so proud of.

We are associated with a number of organizations and work for the welfare of the people, culture, and nature in Nepal. Take a different perspective on travel with us. Travel with un-matchable suave and style with Nepal Ventures!
